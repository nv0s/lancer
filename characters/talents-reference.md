# Talents Reference (Core Rulebook)

All 28 talents, full rank I / II / III text.

## Ace
*Every pilot brags about their abilities...*
- **I — Acrobatics:** While flying, make all AGILITY checks/saves with +1 accuracy. Any time an attack misses you while flying, you may fly up to 2 spaces as a reaction.
- **II — Afterburners:** When you BOOST while flying, move an additional 1d6 spaces, but take heat equal to half that amount.
- **III — Supersonic:** As a quick action while flying, spin up your thrusters; if you end your turn flying, nominate a character within Range = your Speed and line of sight, gaining a reaction: when their turn ends, you fly to a space adjacent to them (ignoring engagement/reactions).

## Bonded
*Everyone should have a friend to watch their back...*
- **I — I'm Your Huckleberry:** Choose a BONDMATE (ideally another PC). Mech skill checks/saves either of you make while adjacent gain +1 accuracy (+2 if both have this talent).
- **II — Sundance:** Reaction, 1/round: when your BONDMATE takes damage from a source in your line of sight while adjacent, you may take the damage instead.
- **III — Cover Me!:** If a character targets your BONDMATE, you can spend OVERWATCH to threaten them into switching targets or eating an immediate OVERWATCH attack.

## Brawler
*Up close and personal...*
- **I — Hold and Lock:** +1 accuracy on melee attacks against targets you're GRAPPLING.
- **II — Sledgehammer:** IMPROVISED ATTACKS gain KNOCKBACK 2 and deal 2d6+2 damage.
- **III — Knockout Blow:** Gain a BRAWLER DIE (d6, starts at 6), reduced by 1 each GRAPPLE/RAM/improvised attack. At 1, reset to 6 and make a full-action knockout blow against an adjacent character (HULL save or take 2d6+2 and STUNNED).

## Brutal
*As fast and messy as possible...*
- **I — Predator:** Natural 20 crits deal maximum possible damage (including bonus damage).
- **II — Cull the Herd:** Critical hits gain KNOCKBACK 1.
- **III — Relentless:** When you miss an attack, your next attack gains +1 accuracy; stacks until you hit.

## Centimane
*The Hundred Hands...*
- **I — Ten Thousand Teeth:** 1/round, crit with a NEXUS forces a SYSTEMS save or target becomes IMPAIRED+SLOWED until end of their next turn.
- **II — Expose Weakness:** Consuming LOCK ON with a NEXUS/DRONE attack and critting makes the target SHREDDED until your next turn.
- **III — Tidal Suppression** (replaces Ten Thousand Teeth): 1/round, crit with a NEXUS forces a SYSTEMS save or choose an effect: Harrying Swarm (Impaired+Slowed), Blinding Swarm (LOS only to adjacent), Virulent Swarm (Shredded, spreads to adjacent enemies), or Restricting Swarm (burn on every action/reaction).

## Combined Arms
*Knowing how to use both...*
- **I — Shield of Blades:** While ENGAGED, you and adjacent allies count as having soft cover.
- **II — CQB-Trained:** You don't gain DIFFICULTY from being ENGAGED.
- **III — Storm of Violence:** Hitting with melee gives +1 accuracy on your next ranged attack vs. that target, and vice versa (doesn't stack).

## Duelist
*An elegance to the way a mech moves...*
- **I — Partisan:** +1 accuracy on the first MAIN MELEE attack each turn.
- **II — Blademaster:** 1/round on a MAIN MELEE hit, gain a BLADEMASTER DIE (max 3). Spend 1-for-1: Parry (reaction, resistance to a melee hit), Deflect (reaction, roll dice vs a ranged hit, 5+ grants resistance), Feint (ignore engagement/reactions moving past a character), Trip (AGILITY save or PRONE).
- **III — Unstoppable:** 1/round on a melee hit, spend a BLADEMASTER DIE to immediately GRAPPLE or RAM as a free action.

## Drone Commander
*The swarm is hers...*
- **I — Shepherd:** DRONE systems gain +5 HP; move one controlled drone within SENSORS up to 4 spaces as a protocol.
- **II — Energized Swarm:** 1/round, an attack consuming LOCK ON triggers a pulse from your drones — 1d6 damage in a 1-space radius around each drone (each character only hit once).
- **III — Invigorate:** Quick action, send a pulse to an allied character (including drones) within Range 3, extendable chain to others within Range 3 of each other: allies gain 4 OVERSHIELD, hostiles in the line take 2 damage.

## Engineer
*A little old-fashioned ingenuity...*
- **I — Prototype:** On a Full Repair, install a prototype weapon (Main, Limited 1d6+2 reroll each repair, Overkill, choose type/damage each repair; counts as integrated mount).
- **II — Revision:** On Full Repair, choose 2: Tweaked Optics (+1 accuracy always), Tweaked Computer (SMART tag), Stripped Reactor Shielding (pay 2 difficulty for altered range/melee profile).
- **III — Final Draft:** Prototype weapon becomes Limited 2d6, deals 1d6+4 damage.

## Executioner
*A clean death in combat...*
- **I — Backswing Cut:** 1/round on a HEAVY/SUPERHEAVY melee hit, make a free follow-up melee attack (half damage) against a different character within threat range and LOS.
- **II — Wide Arc Cleave:** First crit each round with a HEAVY/SUPERHEAVY melee weapon deals 3 damage to all other characters/objects within threat range.
- **III — No Escape:** 1/round, missing with melee lets you reroll against a different target within threat range and LOS.

## Exemplar
*By words and by action...*
- **I — Honorable Challenge:** First attack each turn vs. a hostile within Range 3 (hit or miss) can apply EXEMPLAR'S MARK (free action, one at a time, replaces on new mark) until start of your next turn. While marked: reaction — ally who attacks your mark and misses may reroll (must keep new result).
- **II — Punishment:** When the marked character attacks anyone within Range 3 of you besides you, it triggers your OVERWATCH.
- **III — To the Death:** Free action when marking, challenge to a duel: you and the marked character get +3 accuracy vs. others until end of scene or either mech is destroyed; broken if they attack you. Can't move away from them; mark persists, no new marks while active.

## Gunslinger
*Justice made whole...*
- **I — Opening Argument:** +1 accuracy on the first attack each turn with an AUXILIARY ranged weapon.
- **II — From the Hip:** Reaction — when hit by a ranged attack, immediately return fire with an AUXILIARY ranged weapon if in range.
- **III — I Kill With My Heart:** Gain a GUNSLINGER DIE (d6, starts at 6), reduced by 1 on each Aux ranged hit. At 1, reset to 6 for +2d6 bonus damage and AP (ignores cover) on your next Aux ranged attack.

## Grease Monkey
*Something spooky about how Eel's beast runs...*
- **I — Unsanctioned Capacity Upgrade:** While resting, spend 2 Repairs to replenish 1 use of all LIMITED weapons/systems.
- **II — Machine Bond:** When you STABILIZE, clear IMPAIRED, JAMMED, IMMOBILIZED, SLOWED, and LOCK ON that weren't caused by your own systems/talents.
- **III — Friends in High Places:** Once per mission while resting, call a supply drop: you and allies replenish 1 use of all LIMITED gear and restore 1 STRUCTURE, free of REPAIR cost, even past REPAIR CAP.

## Hacker *(Lee has Rank I)*
*Han wins fights without firing a single shot...*
- **I — Snow_Crash:** Hitting with a tech attack that consumes LOCK ON forces the target to choose: take 2 heat, or be pushed 3 spaces.
- **II — Safe_Cracker:** New INVADE options — Jam Cockpit (can't mount/dismount until fixed), Disable Life Support (+1 difficulty on all saves until rebooted), Hack./Slash (can't take tech actions until shut down/rebooted).
- **III — Last Argument of Kings:** New Full Tech option — tech attack within SENSORS/LOS; on success, target takes burn equal to current heat (resolves before their heat cap reset if it causes overheat).

## Heavy Gunner
*Time to get to work...*
- **I — Covering Fire:** Quick action, target within LOS/range of a HEAVY ranged weapon (within 10 spaces) becomes IMPAIRED until your next turn or until they move more than 1 space (which lets you make a reaction attack at half damage with that weapon, ending the effect). Replacing the target ends the previous one; ends if the target damages you.
- **II — Hammerbeat:** If your COVERING FIRE reaction attack hits, the target becomes IMMOBILIZED until end of their next turn.
- **III — Bracketing Fire:** COVERING FIRE can target two characters at once, resolved/tracked separately.

## Hunter
*Silent, quick, and controlled...*
- **I — Lunge:** 1/round on an AUXILIARY melee attack, fly up to 3 spaces directly toward the target first (ignores engagement/reactions).
- **II — Knife Juggler:** AUXILIARY melee weapons gain (or improve to) THROWN 5; thrown weapons used this turn automatically return to you at end of turn.
- **III — Disdainful Blade:** 1/round on a melee hit, also throw an AUXILIARY melee weapon as a free-action attack (no bonus damage) against anyone in range.

## Infiltrator *(Lee has Rank I)*
*Never seen unless you want to be...*
- **I — Prowl:** On your turn: entering LOS or leaving cover doesn't break HIDDEN; you can pass through (not end in) enemy spaces; you can HIDE in plain sight. Ends at end of turn.
- **II — Ambush:** When you start your turn HIDDEN, your first attack roll forces a HULL save on the target or they become SLOWED, IMPAIRED, and can't take reactions until end of their next turn.
- **III — Mastermind:** When you lose HIDDEN, you may first fire a flash bomb at an adjacent character (SYSTEMS save or LOS limited to adjacent spaces until their next turn), then move up to your speed ignoring engagement/reactions before being revealed.

## Juggernaut
*A couple tons of metal powered by a sliver of dying star...*
- **I — Momentum:** After BOOST, your next RAM before your next turn gets +1 accuracy and 2 extra knockback spaces.
- **II — Kinetic Mass Transfer:** 1/round, RAMing a target into another character forces a HULL save or PRONE; into an obstruction deals 1d6 damage to your target.
- **III — Unstoppable Force:** 1/round on BOOST, supercharge: move max speed in a straight line, take 1d3+3 damage, pass through same-size-or-smaller characters (HULL save or PRONE), smash through terrain (20 AP damage to stop you), ignore difficult terrain, ignore engagement/reactions.

## Leader
*The Old Man of the battlefield...*
- **I — Field Commander:** Gain 3 LEADERSHIP DICE (d6). 1/turn free action, give an order + a die to an ally; they spend it for +1 accuracy on the resulting action, or return it. Allies hold 1 die max; dice last until used or scene end.
- **II — Open Channels:** 5 Leadership Dice instead of 3; can issue commands as a reaction on any player's turn, any number of times per round.
- **III — Inspiring Presence:** 6 Leadership Dice instead of 5; allies can spend dice to reduce incoming damage by 1d6 or add 1d6 bonus damage on a hit.

## Nuclear Cavalier
*Why not open that compartment up...*
- **I — Aggressive Heat Bleed:** First attack each turn while in the DANGER ZONE deals +2 accuracy on a hit (delayed to next attack if you enter Danger Zone mid-turn).
- **II — Fusion Hemorrhage:** First ranged/melee attack in the Danger Zone deals heat damage instead of its normal type, plus +1d6 bonus damage on hit.
- **III — Here, Catch!:** Gain an integrated Fuel Rod Gun (Main CQB, Limited 3, [R3][Blast 3][1d3+2], clears 4 heat on attack).

## Siege Specialist
*No wall can withstand you...*
- **I — Jackhammer:** Quick action, if you have a CANNON: fire a jackhammer round dealing 10 AP damage to a Size 1 section of any object in range (cover, terrain, deployables); adjacent characters knocked back 2 spaces and take 2 damage.
- **II — Impact:** 1/round before a CANNON attack roll, adjacent characters must succeed on a HULL save or be knocked back 1 space and PRONE; you're pushed 1 space in any direction.
- **III — Collateral Damage:** 1/round on a CANNON crit, trigger a 2-space explosion: characters in the area drop PRONE as a reaction or take 2 damage and knockback 2.

## Skirmisher
*Stay low, stay mobile, stay fast...*
- **I — Integrated Chaff Launchers:** Gain soft cover at the start of your turn; lost if you attack or force a save.
- **II — Lockbreaker:** Before/after you SKIRMISH (including as a reaction), move 2 spaces ignoring engagement/reactions.
- **III — Weave:** The first reaction-attack made against you each round automatically misses.

## Spotter
*To see all...*
- **I — Particularize:** When an adjacent ally attacks a target with LOCK ON, they roll twice and keep the better result.
- **II — Panopticon:** At end of turn, if you didn't move and used LOCK ON (Quick Tech), gain a free LOCK ON; when you LOCK ON, learn the target's Armor, Speed, Evasion, E-Defense, mech skills, and current HP (shareable with allies).
- **III — Bentham/Foucault Elimination:** Quick action when you LOCK ON, an adjacent ally may immediately take any quick action as a reaction, consuming the target's LOCK ON (benefits apply if they attack).

## Stormbringer
*Drops of water can carve away the earth...*
- **I — Seismic Deluge:** 1/round, a successful LAUNCHER attack consuming LOCK ON can also knock the target PRONE.
- **II — Stormbending:** 1/round on a LAUNCHER hit, choose Lightning (HULL save or knocked back 3, you knocked back 3 too) or Thunder (2-space burst, AGILITY save or knockback 1, primary target unaffected).
- **III — Torrent:** Gain a TORRENT DIE (d6, starts at 6), reduced by 1 each use of Stormbending. At 1, reset to 6 for a full-action massive attack (Range 15): AGILITY save or 2d6 damage + STUNNED + PRONE; success halves damage, still PRONE.

## Tactician
*Could read the field as easy as a book...*
- **I — Opportunist / Solar Backdrop:** 1/round, +1 accuracy on a melee attack if an ally is ENGAGED with your target, OR +1 accuracy on a ranged attack made from higher elevation than your target.
- **II — Overlapping Fire:** Reaction — when an allied attack hits a character who doesn't have cover from your position, you may OVERWATCH them for half damage.
- **III** *(not fully captured — ask for detail if picked)*

## Technophile
*Let your NHP cascade...*
- **I — Servant Fragment:** Gain a custom Servant-Class NHP (0 SP, AI tag) — talks, has personality, but no independent thought; follows last instruction and defends itself.
- **II — Student Fragment:** Upgrades to Student-Class NHP — independent thought/judgment; 1/round, reroll any mech skill check or save (must keep new result).
- **III — Enlightenment:** Installed AIs can't cascade unless you allow it; cascading/unshackled NHPs no longer view you with indifference. Upgrades to Enlightenment-Class NHP — doesn't count toward AI limit, benefits from your talents, can be carried outside your mech; 1/round reroll any mech skill check or save (must keep new result).

## Vanguard
*Get through their guard, get in their face...*
- **I — Handshake Etiquette:** +1 accuracy with CQB weapons against targets within Range 3.
- **II — See-Through Seeker:** Ignore soft and hard cover with CQB weapons against targets within Range 3, given line of sight.
- **III — Semper Vigilo:** Can OVERWATCH-attack with CQB weapons when hostiles enter, leave, or move within your threat range, regardless of where their movement started.

## Walking Armory
*Lay waste in style, and live forever...*
- **I — Armament:** Gain an Ammo Case system (0 SP, Limited 6). 1/turn on a MAIN ranged attack, spend charges for effects: Thumper (1 charge, knockback 1 + explosive damage type), Shock (1 charge, energy damage + 1 AP to adjacent characters whether hit or miss), Mag (1 charge, gains ARCING + explosive damage).
- **II — Expanded Portfolio:** New 2-charge ammo types: Hellfire (energy damage, bonus damage also becomes energy), Jager (knockback 2, explosive damage, HULL save or PRONE), Sabot (AP + kinetic damage).
- **III — Efficiency:** Critical hits using Ammo Case ammunition don't expend charges (multi-target attacks only get this on the first attack roll).
