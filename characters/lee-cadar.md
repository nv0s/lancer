# Lee "Envy" Cadar

**License Level:** LL0
**Background:** Mercenary
**Grit:** 0 (first point at LL1)

6'4", lean, bald, bearded. Red mechanic's jumpsuit with light plating underneath. Never without his aviators. Cocky, sharp, allergic to being seen unless he wants to be.

Grew up on a nameless backwater world; left the day the first merc outfit that touched dirt would have him. Never looked back. Reputation: the best shot this side of the Core worlds — and he'll be the first to tell you so.

- **Act Unseen or Unheard** — move, hide, or operate without detection
- **Hack or Fix** — break into or repair electronic systems
- **Show Off** — do something flashy or impressive, usually with a gun
