# The Squad

Lee's team — a small independent mercenary outfit operating on the frontier. Full pilot and mech sheets, LL2. Each has a distinct build identity that GM leveling leans further into over time, rather than mirroring Lee's picks.

- [Voss "Doc" Amara](voss-amara.md) — Support / healer (IPS-N Lancaster): repair drones and battlefield triage, backed by Grease Monkey and a Bonded tie to Lee.
- [Kessa "Bull" Draye](kessa-draye.md) — Tank / defender (IPS-N Drake): highest HP in the crew, hard cover and shield-generator damage mitigation, Combined Arms + Juggernaut for holding a line.
- [Jonas "Riot" Achebe](jonas-achebe.md) — DPS / striker (IPS-N Nelson): fast, evasive melee brawler, Duelist + Skirmisher for in-and-out damage.

## Group Dynamic
This isn't a family — it's a crew that's stayed together because the work has been good and nobody's died yet. Doc keeps them alive and honest. Bull keeps them disciplined. Riot keeps them moving. Lee keeps them paid and, so far, keeps them unseen when it matters. How long that holds is an open question — this is a gritty, political setting, and contracts on the frontier rarely stay clean.
