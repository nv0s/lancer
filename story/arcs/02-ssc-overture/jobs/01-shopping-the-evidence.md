# Job 1: Shopping the Evidence

**Arc:** [The SSC Overture](../arc.md)
**Status:** Active
**Contact:** Priya Okafor, SSC regional procurement liaison

Two days after the Vantage depot fell, with the crew resting up in Rill's End and Sato working through the recovered materiel, Lee decided the HA flight recorder and manifests were worth more as leverage than as a trophy. Word through old merc-network contacts found a buyer fast: Smith-Shimano Corpro, a bitter rival of Harrison Armory, very interested in documentary proof HA armed an illegal frontier tariff racket.

## The Meet
Neutral ground, a trade station two jumps out. Okafor already had the lot numbers cross-referenced before the meet started — she wasn't there to be convinced the evidence was real, only to negotiate price. Her opening offer: partial evidence for partial hardware, full disclosure for a real trade — potentially including a rail weapon system, since proof like this is worth real money to SSC's legal and PR departments in whatever fight they're planning against HA.

Lee opted for full reveal and tried to sell the deal by leaning on reputation (Show Off, 1d20+2 = 6 vs. DC 15 — missed). Okafor wasn't moved by theatrics; if anything it read as a tell that he needed the sale more than the goods warranted. She held the hardware offer where it was but added a condition: she needed the file to hold up in a room with lawyers, which meant a name and a location — not an anonymous frontier dot.

Lee gave her Rill's End: Sato, the co-op, all of it. Deal closed clean.

## Outcome
- **Traded away:** HA flight recorder + shipping manifests (full evidence set).
- **Received:** SSC military-spec Rail Gun — the same weapon system that arms the Death's Head. Swapped into Ungoliant's Heavy mount in place of the Anti-Materiel Rifle (crated for resale). See [`lee-cadar.md`](../../../../characters/lee-cadar.md).
- **Consequence:** Rill's End is now a named party in SSC's case file against Harrison Armory. Real backing if that escalates — but also a name HA will eventually connect to their missing evidence. Tracked in [`open-threads.md`](../../../open-threads.md).
