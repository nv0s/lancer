# Arc: The SSC Overture

**Status:** Complete
**Squad:** Lee "Envy" Cadar (pilot, mech *Ungoliant*), Doc, Bull, Riot

## Hook
Fresh off the Vantage depot's fall, the crew is sitting on hard documentary proof that Harrison Armory knowingly armed Cassian Vantage Security — a flight recorder and shipping manifests, both worth real money to the right buyer. Rather than sit on it or hand it to a colony court with no teeth, Lee goes looking for a corporate buyer with an axe to grind against HA.

## Summary
Lee brokered the HA evidence to Smith-Shimano Corpro. A missed Show Off roll cost him leverage on price, and Okafor held firm on a full-disclosure deal that named Rill's End (Sato, the co-op) in SSC's case file against Harrison Armory. In exchange, SSC handed over a military-spec rail gun — swapped into *Ungoliant*'s Heavy mount in place of the Anti-Materiel Rifle.
