# Job 1: The Halvorsen Escort

**Arc:** [The Corvo Reach Run](../arc.md)
**Status:** Complete
**Contact:** Nemo Halvorsen, Halvorsen Freight Concern

## The Contract
Escort the freighter *Long Ledger* through the Corvo Reach, a piracy-plagued stretch between two blink gates. Flat pay up front, completion bonus for zero cargo loss and zero crew casualties on either side of the contract. Intel was thin going in — Halvorsen didn't know what they were actually facing.

## The Ambush
Two jumps in without incident; on the third, four raider craft dropped out of a debris field ambush position. No warning, no stealth setup on the crew's side this time — first time the party's been on the receiving end of an ambush instead of running one.

**Opposition:** a Raider Captain (leader-tier, Heavy Melee) plus three Corvo Raiders (mook-tier, Main Cannon). Stats logged in [`bestiary.md`](../../../../reference/bestiary.md).

Lee opened on the Captain and (after a player-requested reroll of the opening exchange) dropped it in a single turn — 9 damage from the Rail Gun plus a follow-up Assault Rifle hit finished it before it acted. Bull planted herself between the raiders and the *Long Ledger* to draw fire, soaking hits off pure armor. Riot's attempts to flank and finish raiders came up empty most of the fight (bad rolls, not bad plan). Doc ran full support: Restock Drone to reload Lee's Rail Gun mid-fight, then a cable-winch trip that pinned one raider prone and immobilized for the rest of the fight — later a clean interrogation target. Round 3 closed it out; freighter delivered at 11/20 HP, no crew losses.

## Interrogation
Same play as the Vantage captures — comply or don't. The pinned raider broke immediately (21 vs. target 10):
- **The hideout:** a stripped hab-carrier in the same debris field the ambush launched from.
- **This wasn't opportunistic.** Halvorsen's shipping schedule was leaked to the raiders in advance, through a paid broker — someone on the inside, or close to it.
- **There's a network behind this cell** — multiple raider crews across the Reach, all kicking a cut up to something the raider only knew by callsign: **"the Undertow."**

## Hideout Salvage
Looted the hab-carrier clean (19 vs. target 10): crates of plunder from prior raids on other freighters (returnable or sellable), a functional kinetic cannon added to the crew's stores, and comms logs confirming the Undertow is an ongoing, real operation — not one dying raider's story.

## Outcome
- **Long Ledger delivered intact.** Full contract payment plus completion bonus from Halvorsen.
- **Loot:** stolen cargo (Halvorsen's to decide what to do with), a spare kinetic cannon (crew stores), comms logs referencing the Undertow.
- **Open thread:** the Undertow — a raider network, and a leak inside (or adjacent to) Halvorsen's own operation. Not chased yet by choice, not because it's resolved.
