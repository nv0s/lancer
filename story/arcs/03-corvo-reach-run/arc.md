# Arc: The Corvo Reach Run

**Status:** Complete
**Squad:** Lee "Envy" Cadar (pilot, mech *Ungoliant*), Doc, Bull, Riot

## Hook
Fresh off the Vantage job and the SSC trade, the crew went looking for straightforward, well-paying work — no politics, no leverage plays, just a contract. **Halvorsen Freight Concern**, a mid-size independent shipping outfit, was offering solid pay for escort through the **Corvo Reach**, a contested stretch of space between two blink gates where piracy had spiked. Three confirmed hits on Halvorsen ships already; thin intel on what was actually out there.

## Summary
A straightforward escort job that stayed straightforward — until the interrogation afterward turned up a thread nobody was looking for.

## Jobs
1. [The Halvorsen Escort](jobs/01-halvorsen-escort.md) — Complete. Ambushed in the Reach by a raider cell; captain and crew destroyed or captured, freighter delivered intact, contract paid in full. Interrogation and hideout salvage turned up "the Undertow" — a raider network bigger than one dead cell.
