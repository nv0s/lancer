# Arc 4: The Undertow

**Status:** Complete
**Contact:** none — self-directed, off intel from the Halvorsen escort

## Hook
A captured raider from the Halvorsen escort gave up more than a hideout: the Corvo Reach's raider cells aren't independent. Every crew kicks a cut up to something called **"the Undertow"** — and the hab-carrier's comms logs confirmed it's a real, ongoing operation, not one dying man's story. Someone leaked Halvorsen Freight's shipping schedule to the raiders in advance, through a paid broker. Whether that's the same organization or a separate problem is still unknown.

No client, no payday guaranteed — this one starts on the crew's own initiative, working off two loose threads: the Undertow's network, and whoever inside (or adjacent to) Halvorsen sold them the *Long Ledger*'s schedule.

## Summary
Cracked the raiders' comms logs to a real lead — a fixer called "the Actuary," real name Wren Osei — and hit their rendezvous at Tallow Point directly. Took Osei alive, stole their ship, and pulled a full client ledger implicating buyers and leaks across the Reach. A live transaction on the console pointed to an ambush already in progress; the crew raced to intercept, arrived mid-boarding instead of before it, and cleaned out the raider cell hitting an independent hauler, *Kestrel's Due*. Closed the loop with Halvorsen Freight Concern: handed over both prisoners, the stolen ship, and the ledger, and used it to identify the actual leak inside Halvorsen's own operation.
