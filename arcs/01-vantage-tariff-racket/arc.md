# Arc: The Vantage Tariff Racket

**Status:** Complete
**Squad:** Lee "Envy" Cadar (pilot, mech *Ungoliant*), Doc, Bull, Riot

## Hook
**Starting location: Rill's End**, a Diasporan mining colony on the outer edge of charted space — tidally locked, one habitable strip, and rich enough in rare ore to be worth fighting over. Officially self-administered. Practically, it's been getting squeezed for two years by **Cassian Vantage Security**, a private contractor running on a Harrison Armory paper trail, who "license" mining claims and charge "transit tariffs" that have no basis in any law Union would recognize — and no Union administrator has shown up to say otherwise.

The crew landed on a contract from a claims cooperative that's tired of paying. It looked like a simple escort/deterrence job. It did not stay simple — Cassian Vantage's muscle was better armed than a rent-a-cop outfit had any business being, and someone was bankrolling that gap.

## Summary
Contract struck with Imogen Sato (claims cooperative): reduced cash retainer + binding claim on whatever's recovered from Cassian Vantage's impound depot. Salvage had no clean title — political risk, not just cash. A Harrison Armory "advisor" was confirmed embedded with Vantage directly, meaning the tariff racket went higher than a local contractor.

The plan: bleed Vantage's patrols over several days (deniable hits, no direct move on the colony) to provoke overextension, then hit the depot. Doc coordinated with Sato throughout to watch for retaliation against colonists. The plan worked — Vantage escalated their response with each hit, which is exactly what let the crew identify and dismantle their real defenses before the final assault.
