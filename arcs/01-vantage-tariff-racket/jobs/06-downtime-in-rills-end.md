# Job 6: Downtime in Rill's End

**Arc:** [The Vantage Tariff Racket](../arc.md)

Full repair on the crew (couple of days, secure downtime). Checked in with Sato: no retaliation against the colony so far. She flagged the depot's resupply convoy (due in ~2 days) as a soft target — cutting it could starve the depot's defenses, possibly including the second outfit's own resupply. Lee dug through local shipping/customs records and identified the second outfit: **"Ossuary Freight"** — a shell company, thin cover, clean-money financing, active alongside Vantage only since the tariff racket escalated. The digging wasn't invisible; Ossuary will eventually know their records were accessed.
