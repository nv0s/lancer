# Job 5: Depot Recon

**Arc:** [The Vantage Tariff Racket](../arc.md)

Lee, solo, night. Salvage attempt on the Enforcer wreck and a trace on the mystery transponder ID both failed; the trace may have tipped off the second outfit that someone's looking into them. Recon itself: got the gate, guard tower, and motor pool layout, and confirmed patrol behavior, but tripped an old proximity sensor (evaded a search) and was later directly spotted by a guard. Killed the guard to avoid a wider alarm, got a glimpse into the hangar before pulling out — a large Harrison Armory-pattern mech, energy-armed, confirmed as the depot's real defender. Escaped clean, but the depot is now alerted that someone was there. No more free ambushes on this target.
