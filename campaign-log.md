# Campaign Log

**System:** LANCER Core Rulebook (Massif Press)
**Tone:** Gritty and political — full setting weight
**Format:** Sandbox. Player: Lee "Envy" Cadar. GM-run squad: Doc, Bull, Riot.

## Setting Primer
5016u. Humanity has spread across the galaxy for millennia, governed at its core by Union — a post-capital utopia trying to live up to its own ideals from a comfortable distance. Outside the Core, the galaxy runs on manna, blink gates, and the omninet, and five major manufacturers (GMS, IPS-Northstar, Smith-Shimano Corpro, HORUS, and Harrison Armory) arm the states, militias, and mercenary companies that fill the gaps Union doesn't reach. The frontier is not utopia. It's contested, exploited, and armed to the teeth.

## Session 0 — Setup (2026-07-20)

**Starting location: Rill's End**, a Diasporan mining colony on the outer edge of charted space — tidally locked, one habitable strip, and rich enough in rare ore to be worth fighting over. Officially self-administered. Practically, it's been getting squeezed for two years by **Cassian Vantage Security**, a private contractor running on a Harrison Armory paper trail, who "license" mining claims and charge "transit tariffs" that have no basis in any law Union would recognize — and no Union administrator has shown up to say otherwise.

The crew has landed on a contract from a claims cooperative that's tired of paying. It looks like a simple escort/deterrence job. It is not going to stay simple — Cassian Vantage's muscle is better armed than a rent-a-cop outfit has any business being, and someone is bankrolling that gap.
