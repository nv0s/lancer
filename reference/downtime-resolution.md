# Downtime Resolution

How downtime between jobs gets run. Established 2026-07-22.

## Structure
Present the actual named downtime actions as an explicit menu when downtime comes up — don't just narrate freeform. One downtime action per downtime period, unless the downtime spans an especially long stretch (months+), per core rules. Triggers can still apply bonuses to a downtime roll where they logically fit (e.g., Survive on GET A DAMN DRINK, Word on the Street for gossip).

Outcomes are always read at three tiers: **9 or less / 10-19 / 20+.**

## The menu (core rulebook downtime actions)
- **POWER AT A COST** — name what you want; you get it, but the GM attaches 1-2 complications (time, risk, something given up, unwanted attention, needing more info/resources first, getting only a lesser version).
- **BUY SOME TIME** — stave off a reckoning or extend a window. 9-: only with drastic measures, or it catches up to you. 10-19: bought, but the situation gets precarious (downgrades to 9- if invoked again for the same problem). 20+: bought clean until next downtime (downgrades to 10-19 if invoked again).
- **GATHER INFORMATION** — investigate a named subject. 9-: get it, but it causes immediate trouble, or you bail and miss it. 10-19: get it, but leave evidence or have to burn/implicate someone. 20+: clean, no complications.
- **GET A DAMN DRINK** — blow off steam, make connections, forget the last job. 9-: good or bad night, but wake up having lost dignity/possessions/memory (pick one). 10-19: gain one from {reputation, connection, item/info, opportunity}, lose one. 20+: gain two, lose nothing. Needs an actual place to do it (town/station/city).
- **GET CREATIVE** — build or tweak something (physical or software) for RESERVES. 9-: no progress (next attempt on same project auto-reads as 10-19). 10-19: progress, not finished — pick two of {quality materials, specific knowledge, specialized tools, a good workspace} needed to finish next time without a roll. 20+: finished (complex projects downgrade to 10-19, pick one).
- **GET FOCUSED** — train a new +2 trigger (non-martial, personal/flavorful) or upgrade an existing one (+2→+4→+6).
- **GET ORGANIZED** — start/grow an organization (military/scientific/academic/criminal/humanitarian/industrial/entertainment/political focus). Starts +2 EFFICIENCY or INFLUENCE, +0 the other, cap +6. Must re-roll every downtime after founding (doesn't cost the downtime action slot). 9-: loses 2/2 (min 0) or must pay debts/make a move/get bailed out. 10-19: +2 to one stat (cap +6). 20+: +2 to both (cap +6).
- **GET CONNECTED** — call in favors/support from a named contact. 9-: they help, but you owe a favor immediately or they back out. 10-19: they help, favor owed after the fact (missed follow-through downgrades future rolls to 9-). 20+: help, no strings (downgrades to 10-19 next time for the same contact). Needs comms range or a face-to-face.
- **SCROUNGE AND BARTER** — track down a physical item/asset (doesn't need to be on the gear list). 9-: get it, but it's stolen/degraded/contested (pick one). 10-19: get it, pick a price (time, dignity, reputation, health/comfort). 20+: clean, no problem.

Custom downtime actions can be created on the fly if something doesn't fit this list — these are a starting point, not the ceiling.
