# GM Reminders

Things that got corrected more than once, or were significant enough to make sure they don't recur. Check this if something feels like it might be a repeat mistake.

## Turn order
Every character on both sides acts exactly **once** per round. Track who's gone this round explicitly before picking the next hostile actor — don't let one NPC act twice while another hasn't gone yet. (Corrected: Vantage Enforcer was mistakenly given two actions in one round during the resupply-route ambush.)

## Pre-combat setup actions are free
Positioning that happens while still concealed/undetected and before the first shot — being dug in with a weapon sighted, deploying passive systems like Turret Drones — doesn't cost a turn's action. This was inconsistent at first (charged an action for turret deployment once, didn't for ridge positioning earlier) — now standardized as free. (Corrected during the resupply convoy ambush.)

## Reaction effects scale per-instance, not globally
"1/round" limits on a reaction (e.g. Turret Drones' Turret Attack) apply **per individual instance** of the system, not once for the whole effect type. Three deployed turrets = three independent 1/round triggers, all of which can fire off the same qualifying hit if each has range/LOS. (Corrected during the resupply convoy ambush — initially missed applying it at all.)

## Proximity triggers vs. remote triggers
A mine/proximity-rigged trap goes off automatically when the trigger condition is met (no action spent by anyone). Only a manually remote-detonated device costs the triggering character an action. Get the player's actual intent on which type they're rigging before assuming. (Corrected during the trap-setting sequence at the tariff route ambush — initially assumed remote-trigger when the player meant proximity.)

## Salvage Mercy (house rule, active)
A failed loot/salvage roll still yields something minor — never a complete whiff. After two failed salvage attempts in a row, the third auto-succeeds at full value, no roll needed. (Adopted after three straight encounters produced no material loot.)
