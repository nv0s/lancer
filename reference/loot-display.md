# Loot & Salvage Display

How loot gets presented after a fight or a search. Established 2026-07-22.

## Salvage Mercy (house rule, active — see `campaign-log.md`)
A failed loot/salvage roll still yields something minor, never a total whiff. After two failed salvage attempts in a row, the third auto-succeeds at full value, no roll needed.

## Presentation
After a fight or search resolves, give an **itemized list** of everything found — named items, quantities, condition/notes where relevant. Then **ask who wants what** before anything goes onto a character sheet: who carries it, who equips it, whether it goes to crew stores instead. Don't auto-assign loot to a sheet without that check, even when the answer seems obvious.
